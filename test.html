Brandy B. Brasket
Breathe in...
breathe out. SMILE.
 Learn something new.
 Treat everyone like family.
 Be optimistic.
 Believe in making the
impossible possible.
   Always try at least
one bite of new food.
 Take responsibility for
your actions  and be mindful
 of consequences.
 Let every word you speak be to the encouragement of the listener.
 If you want to SWING at the park,
 don't let being an adult stop you.
 Find a good book to read.
 Listen to someone
whose thoughts are nothing like yours.
 Be willing to feel uncomfortable.
 be humble,     but don't forget
     you are awesome.

 Your existence is a miracle.

 Above all, show love to everyone.
